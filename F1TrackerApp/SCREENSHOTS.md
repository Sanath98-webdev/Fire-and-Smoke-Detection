## Design Highlights

### Liquid Glass Effect
- Ultra-thin material backgrounds
- Subtle blur effects
- Gradient stroke overlays (white with opacity)
- Layered depth with shadows
- Smooth corner rounding

### Color Themes by Screen
- **Races**: Red/Black gradients
- **Stats**: Blue/Purple gradients
- **Live**: Green/Blue gradients

### Interactive Elements
- Segmented controls
- Navigation links
- Buttons with color fills
- Tap targets with hover states

### Typography
- System font with varied weights
- Clear hierarchy (title > headline > body > caption)
- White text with opacity variations
- Accent colors for highlights

### Animations
- Smooth tab transitions
- Pulsing LIVE indicator
- Card hover effects
- Gradient animations
